What is DerbyHacks?

Derbyhacks is the culmination of a lot of work and a lot of love for hacking. A weekend long invention competition designed to bring lots of people together for a great time of learning, laughing and building.

FAQ

Some frequently asked questions

Who can come

Any and all college students are allowed to come, no experience required!

Where will it be?

DerbyHacks will be in louisville kentucky

How Long will it be

DerbyHacks will be a 36 hour hackathon

Max team size?

You can fly solo or work with a team of up to 4 people

What should I bring?

At minimum bring a laptop, a phone, relevant chargers, various toiletries, deodorant, and if you want them, sleeping bags/pillows, and anything else you may want or need for the weekend.

Will there be swag?

OF COURSE THERE WILL BE SWAG!!!!
